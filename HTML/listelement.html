Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

for the dough:

  • 3/4 cup wrm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

for the filling:

  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened

Instructions:

  1. Warm the milk to about 110 degrees F (43 degrees C), then stir in the yeast and 1 tablespoon of the sugar. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes, until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the yeast mixture with the egg, egg yolk, remaining sugar, and melted butter. Gradually add the bread flour, mixing until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place it in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface into a rectangle about 16x12 inches.
  5. Spread the softened butter over the dough. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon, then sprinkle this mixture evenly over the buttered dough.
  6. Starting from one of the long edges, tightly roll up the dough into a log. Cut the log into 12 equal slices and place them in a greased baking dish.
  7. Cover the dish with a damp cloth and let the rolls rise for another 30 minutes. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade cinnamon rolls!
